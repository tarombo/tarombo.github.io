Get Started

Tarombo is a free smartphone app to record your genealogy. By logging in with a free GitHub account, you can record and manage together your extended family tree. So, you can share the genealogy that you created with other people so that other people can also add or correct the data. The changes you make can be accepted by them, and the changes they make can be reviewed and accepted for inclusion in your genealogy.
