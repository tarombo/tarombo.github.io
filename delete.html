About your account and data

Tarombo app uses GitHub and does not have its own account system. So when you log in, you log in to GitHub, not to Tarombo app. Tarombo app therefore has no knowledge of your password. Tarombo app stores your genealogy data on your device, as well as on the server on your GitHub account if you choose to do so in the app.

Deleting your data

You can delete your data stored on your device at anytime by deleting all the trees in the app, or you can also delete the app itself, which will delete all your data from your device. If you uploaded your tree to the server, it is located in your account on GitHub's server. Tarombo app does not own the data, and you can delete the trees either via the app, or by going to your GitHub account and delete the corresponding repository. If you only use your GitHub account for Tarombo app, you can also delete your GitHub account and therefore delete all your GitHub data.

Deleting your account

Tarombo app uses GitHub and does not have your account. To delete your account that you use to login, you need to go to GitHub and delete your GitHub account. You can find detailed instruction on how to delete your GitHub account here.
